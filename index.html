Pie-Raydle

This game is a simplified version of pie-raying techniques from Minecraft speedrunning.

Use on-screen buttons to increase or decrease detection radius and move.

Or use WASD/arrow keys to move, F to increase radius and Shift+F to decrease radius.

Welcome to Pie-raydle Be shown where the red target is immediately to help you wrap your head around the system/training tool. Have your radius visualised, and see which squares are candidates (in orange) or definitely empty (in grey). See nothing but the grid, and still try to find that target square.
